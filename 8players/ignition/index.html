acodcha's StarCraft Maps

Ignition

Eight players are squeezed onto a tiny-sized 64x64 map! The teams consist of the four northeast players versus the four southwest players. Each team features a large base shared by its four players. Each shared base contains only two expansions, and both of these expansions are exposed, so coordinate with your team members. A wide central bridge and two narrower side bridges link your team's base to the opposing team's base, though neutral structures must be destroyed to cross the bridges. Control of these three bridges is key!

Select the "Use Map Settings" game type for a 4vs4 team game or for a cooperative game against computers.

Compatible with both StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered.
