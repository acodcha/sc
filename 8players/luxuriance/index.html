Luxuriance

Eight players compete on a large-sized 192x192 map. The teams consist of the four northeast players versus the four southwest players. Each player has their own base on high ground and access to a natural expansion on the low ground in front of their base. Third expansions ring the map on secluded high ground plateaus between each player's base. Fourth expansions dot the high-ground cliffs around the central ruins; these expansions are more exposed, but holding their high ground is advantageous. Fifth expansions are also available but are scattered throughout the central ruins and are highly exposed. Finally, additional mineral-only expansions occupy the four corners of the map; these expansions are the most secluded and are only accessible via air, but they are very small and do not offer any vespene gas. Controlling the central ruins is key, but these ruins are extensive, so coordinate with your team!

Select the "Use Map Settings" game type for a 4vs4 team game or for a cooperative game against computers, or the "Free For All" game type for an 8-player free-for-all game.

Compatible with both StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered.
