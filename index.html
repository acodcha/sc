acodcha's StarCraft Maps

Custom-made maps for the StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered video games by Blizzard Entertainment

This website contains my custom-made maps for the StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered video games by Blizzard Entertainment. Maps used in professional tournaments are typically designed for two-player games, whereas my maps focus on larger player counts.

To play a map, simply download its .scx file and place it anywhere in either of the two StarCraft maps directories on your system, which are typically:

  • C:\Users\Username\Documents\StarCraft\Maps\
  • C:\Program Files (x86)\StarCraft\Maps\

My Battle.net BattleTag is acodcha#1294. If you see me online, drop by and say hello!
