Eclectic Defense

Supports one to six players and four difficulties: easy, moderate, hard, and brutal. The chosen difficulty remains balanced regardless of the number of players. Choose from among seven factions: Terran Infantry, Terran Mech, Terran Airforce, Zerg Hunters, Zerg Flyers, Protoss Warriors, and Protoss Fleet. Increasingly fast and resilient enemy forces periodically spawn at the top of the map and make their way around the map, with enemy ground forces moving clockwise around the map, and enemy air forces moving counterclockwise. Produce defenders and destroy the enemy forces before they reach their destination! Can you win without losing a single life?

Select the "Use Map Settings" game type.

Compatible with both StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered.
