acodcha's StarCraft Maps

Zerg Obstacle Course

Supports 1 to 7 players. Morph into increasingly powerful Zerg strains and be the first to reach the end of the obstacle course! How quickly can you win? Can you win without losing a single unit?

Select the "Use Map Settings" game type.

Compatible with both StarCraft: Brood War and StarCraft: Remastered.
